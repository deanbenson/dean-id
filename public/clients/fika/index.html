What is fika?

A moment to slow down, connect and share.

In Sweden, fika is a daily ritual: time taken with others over coffee, something sweet and good conversation.

That same spirit runs through everything here. From the menu to the interiors, every detail is considered, so the space feels calm, warm and welcoming from first coffee to last cocktail.

Speciality coffee & homemade cinnamon buns Brunch, salads & sharing plates Cocktails & bar snacks till late
Our team

The people who make it Fika.

Say hello when you're in. There's a good chance they already know your order.

Michael Jones

Founder & Owner

Fika is owned and run by Michael, who has spent his working life in hospitality, from senior roles with a UK leisure group to running his own consultancy, MJ Hospitality Services. In May 2025 he took the keys to 7 High Street and set about building the place he'd always wanted for Norton.

Plenty of people see the finished version of Fika. What you don't see is the thought, care and attention behind the space, the atmosphere and the experience. That part is Michael.

Harry

Front of House

If you've visited recently, chances are you've already met Harry. From helping shape the new menus to making sure every guest feels welcome, he's been a huge part of bringing the next chapter of Fika to life.
