Cookies and similar technologies

When you first visit, a banner lets you accept or reject non-essential cookies. We use Google Analytics and Google Ads (loaded through Google Tag Manager) to understand our traffic and measure our advertising. These only run if you choose "Accept all"; if you reject, no analytics or advertising cookies are set. A small number of strictly necessary items needed to make the site work are always allowed.

You can change your choice at any time using the "Cookie settings" link in the website footer. For more on cookies and your rights, see the ICO's guidance at ico.org.uk.
