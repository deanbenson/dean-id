The room

Bigger on the inside.

Behind a painted blue-grey shopfront on the south side of Hutton Rudby, the room opens into deep green walls, a botanical mural, dark walnut floors and two mustard mid-century chairs. A scattering of palms, brass details, framed bird prints and a faint wash of pink neon at the back. There’s a lot more room in here than the front lets on.

It’s small on purpose. One or two guests at a time, no rush, and stylists who are properly trained and take the work seriously. Most appointments come by recommendation and stay for years.
