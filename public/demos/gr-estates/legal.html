Privacy Policy

G.R. Estates ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what personal information we collect, how we use it, and your rights under UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018.

What we collect

When you contact us, request a valuation, book a viewing or make an enquiry, we collect details such as your name, email address, phone number, and the property or address you're interested in.

How we use it

  • To respond to your enquiry and provide our services
  • To arrange valuations, viewings, sales and tenancies
  • To meet our legal and regulatory obligations

Our lawful bases are your consent, the performance of a contract, and our legitimate interests in running the business.

Sharing & retention

We only share your data with trusted suppliers who help us deliver our services (for example referencing, deposit protection and conveyancing partners), and where required by law. We keep your information only for as long as necessary.

Your rights

You can ask us to access, correct or delete your data, or object to how we use it. To do so, contact us using the details below. You also have the right to complain to the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

Cookie Policy

Cookies are small files stored on your device. We use essential cookies to make the site work, and — only with your consent — analytics cookies to understand how the site is used so we can improve it.

  • Essential cookies — required for the site to function. Always on.
  • Analytics cookies — help us measure and improve the site. Only set with your consent.

You can accept or decline non-essential cookies using the banner when you arrive, and change your mind at any time via your browser settings.

Complaints Procedure

We aim to provide the highest standards of service to every client. If something has gone wrong, please tell us so we can put it right.

  • Put your complaint in writing to us at the address below, or call 01642 378022.
