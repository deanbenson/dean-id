Meet the florist

Hello, I'm Lily Calvert

I have loved flowers for as long as I can remember. What started as buckets of blooms on the kitchen table is now Lily in Bloom, a little studio in Skelton where every arrangement is made around you, never pulled off a shelf.

I work with the freshest seasonal stems I can find, favouring soft, garden-gathered shapes and colours that feel collected rather than arranged. Whether it's a single posy to say thinking of you or the flowers that line a wedding aisle, I pour the same care into every tie.
